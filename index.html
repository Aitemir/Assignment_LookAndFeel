National Health Data Collection

The NHDC organization got its start 10 years ago. At NHDC, we are trying to collect the surveys of patients who are fighting cancer in top hospitals of
the country. We work with thousands of patients across the country asking them to provide details and measure customer satisfaction. The results of
our survey help millions of people who have cancer and hospitals that are trying to achieve success as well as bring value to the patients.

In order to participate in the survey, please fill out the the form and click submit under the survey tab.
